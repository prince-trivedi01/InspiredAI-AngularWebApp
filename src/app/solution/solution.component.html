How does RedEye work?
Any CCTV camera only records a movie whenever a theft or damage of property occurs. It only proves to be efficient when security personnel is present near a monitor to examine. RedEye is a product backed with Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision. So, when connected to a CCTV system, it examines human behaviour and detects thefts and other suspicious behaviour to alert you within seconds!
Live Feed
RedEye will monitor your live feed to collect data and analyze it. It will follow up with every human movement to detect suspicious behavior and activities.
Cloud Real Time
There are various CCTV cameras on your premises. You might have a question as to how the software will run so many live feeds in real-time? We use Cloud Based Servers that create a thread to run our live feed and alert you of theft and suspicious behaviour in seconds.
RedEye
RedEye keeps an eye on your live feed, uploads the data on the cloud and through these servers backed by Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision, detects thefts and suspicious behavior. So you can sleep and work in peace!
