Convolutional Neural Network
Using CNN in deep learning, we analyse visual imagery to detect suspicious behaviour. Not just suspicious behaviour, we will also detect faces, objects and human behaviour to analyse and store data which helps RedEye self-learn and increase its efficiency through each passing day.
Cloud-Based Live Feed
The RedEye live feed will be directly connected to cloud. Our deep learning model will use this live feed as data to learn about human behaviour and intern any suspicious activities detected will be prompted through alerts to the customers on the Personal computer and Mobile applications.
